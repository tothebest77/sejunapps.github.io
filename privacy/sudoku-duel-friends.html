Sudoku Duel Friends Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Sudoku Duel Friends" developed and operated by Sejun Studio ("Developer").

The app does not directly request users to manually enter personal information such as name, address, or phone number. However, the app uses third-party services (e.g., Firebase and AdMob) that may automatically collect certain information for functionality, analytics, stability, and advertising.

1. Information We Collect

The app itself does not directly collect personal information through custom forms. However, third-party services integrated in the app may collect information such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version)
  • App usage data (e.g., interactions, performance events)
  • Crash and diagnostics data
  • Advertising identifiers and ad performance data (for ads and measurement)
  • Push notification token (for delivering game-related notifications)

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud data storage, Crash/Diagnostics, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising and ad performance measurement)

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. For more information, please refer to:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Information

Information processed via third-party services may be used to:

  • Provide core app features (e.g., login, cloud sync, multiplayer features)
  • Operate and improve app stability and performance (e.g., crash analysis)
  • Analyze usage trends to improve user experience
  • Display ads and measure ad performance

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell users’ personal information. Data may be shared with third-party service providers (such as Google) solely for providing the services described above.

5. Data Retention

Data collected by third-party services is retained according to the policies and settings of those services. The Developer does not control the retention period of data stored and processed by those providers.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app and third-party services. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
