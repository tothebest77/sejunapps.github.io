Sudoku Duel Friends Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application "Sudoku Duel Friends" developed and operated by Taehun Kim ("Developer").

The app does not directly collect or store personal information from users. However, it uses third-party services to provide core functionality and advertisements.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: authentication, cloud data storage, app stability and analytics
  • Google AdMob: advertising and ad performance measurement

Through these services, certain data such as device information, advertising identifiers, and app usage data may be automatically collected and processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

For more information, please refer to:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

This policy is effective as of November 13, 2025.
